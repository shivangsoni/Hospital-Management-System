OUR MOTO

Our mission to help mankind live healthier and longer. This begins with Practo Search – our product that helps you find the right healthcare practitioner.

The Practo Relevance Algorithm (PRA) is central to our search product and determines the order in which the search results are shown to each consumer for his/her specific query. PRA has been designed from the ground up to adhere to our ‘Patient First’ philosophy and has a singular goal – to provide the most relevant results to your query so that we can help you find the right healthcare practitioner with ease.

Location

Since each listing on Practo has been physically verified by our team, we usually have the accurate location for each of them. When you put a search query, our system recognizes the locality and assigns higher importance to doctors within a 5 km diameter. To give you choice, we also automatically show popular doctors in the neighbouring area.

Specialization

We list doctors from over 200 specialties and sub-specialties recognised by relevant governing bodies. Any specialization listed on a doctor’s profile on Practo is verified by ensuring that the concerned doctor possesses valid qualifications, making him/ her eligible to publish the said specialization. This further ensures that you see the specialists only relevant to your query. This specialization-qualification mapping is governed by our central repository of medical information

Appointment Experience

We also believe that you should be going to healthcare practitioners who provide a great overall healthcare experience. We measure this by analyzing your waiting time, availability of the practitioner, and of course, patient feedback.

User Interest

Appointments: If a lot of users end up selecting a particular doctor to book an appointment with, then we believe that doctor is popular and thus grant a few bonus points in our algorithm, which may positively impact the listing order.

Views: We also place importance on the number of views a doctor profile receives in a 30-day period. We separately measure views received via searches outside of Practo as well as clicks on the profile, post search query on Practo.
